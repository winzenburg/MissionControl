## Messaging Best Practices

### Do:
✅ Lead with transformation, not execution  
✅ Use "architect" framing (strategic, systematic)  
✅ Show concrete examples (Cultivate, kinetic-ui, Kinlet)  
✅ Reference Hedgehog concept naturally  
✅ Demonstrate AI fluency through your own work  
✅ Ask strategic questions (shows systems thinking)  

### Don't:
❌ Position yourself as "just another design ops person"  
❌ List tools/skills without context  
❌ Sound like you're job hunting out of necessity  
❌ Use generic design language without AI-transformation framing  
❌ Forget to demonstrate you're actively building with AI

## Customization Guide

For each outreach, customize these elements:

1. **[Specific AI feature/product]** - Research what they're building (1-2 min LinkedIn/company blog)
2. **Company stage context** - AI-native? Transforming? Enterprise scaling?
3. **Your relevant background** - Healthcare → Salesforce, Fintech → Stripe/Plaid, etc.
4. **Outcome focus** - What transformation would you architect for them specifically?

**Time investment:** 5-10 minutes per message to customize = 10x response rate vs. generic outreach
